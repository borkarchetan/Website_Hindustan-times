Frequently Asked Questions

Can you tell me more about Hindustan Times ?

Hindustan Times is one of India's leading news publications. It is the second most read daily newspaper in India (source: IRS 2019). Representative of an integrated newsroom, www.hindustantimes.com is HT's online portal and is among the fastest growing news websites in India (source: comscore 2019). Hindustan Times provides daily dose of national news, global news, information, entertainment and detailed coverage of significant events. The site has evolved multimedia features like videos, podcasts and slideshows

Why should I subscribe to Hindustan Times e-paper ?

To get full access to Hindustan Times e-paper across devices, you must subscribe to Hindustan Times e-paper in order to read the complete e-paper. As a subscriber, you will be able to download, access archives and read all editions of Hindustan Times newspaper in digital format

What are the benefits of becoming a subscriber ?

As a subscriber, you get the following benefits:
1. Unlimited access to Hindustan Times e-paper across any device
2. Access to archives
3. Ability to download complete edition
4. Option to search, save and share articles form e-paper

How do I subscribe to the Hindustan Times

Activating Hindustan Times e-paper subscription is a 3 step process:
Step1: Choose the subscription plan of your choice for Hindustan Times e-paper

Step2: Sign up on the Hindustan Times website or mobile site using your email id. You will be required to verify your email via OTP and can set your password.

Step3: Make the payment via credit card, debit card, netbanking, American Express, mobile wallets and your subscription will be active. (Hindustan Times subscribers will be redirected to the home page to continue reading). You will receive an activation email in your inbox.
